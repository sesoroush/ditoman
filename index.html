Questions

Ditoman is built around understanding. These questions explain the structure behind the project.

What is Ditoman?

Ditoman is a structured learning and community network focused on technology, energy, infrastructure, economy, media, artificial intelligence, and the systems shaping real life.

Why does TMN exist?

TMN exists as a utility layer inside the Ditoman ecosystem. Its purpose is to connect participation, contribution, access, recognition, and value over time.

Why was a token burn completed?

The first on chain burn was completed as part of long term structural alignment. It was not designed as a short term market move.

What does liquidity mean?

Liquidity helps users access and exchange a token more easily. In Ditoman, liquidity is approached gradually and responsibly.

What is contract verification?

Contract verification makes the source code publicly visible on chain explorers such as BscScan. It is an important step for transparency and trust.
